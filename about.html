Our Story

At CollegePur, we believe every student deserves a clear path to success. Our journey began in 2022 as CollegeYoda, providing expert student guidance.

In 2023, we became a trusted educational consultancy. In 2024, a key conversation inspired bridging academic learning with real-world experience.

In June 2024, we launched our internship program. Rebranding as CollegePur in July reaffirmed our commitment to aspiring minds
